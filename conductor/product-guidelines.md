# Product Guidelines

## Tone and Style
- **Professional & Technical:** Documentation and user-facing content should prioritize clarity, accuracy, and detailed technical explanations.
- **Friendly & Encouraging:** Maintain an approachable tone to instill confidence in users working with autonomous agents.
- **Concise & Direct:** Provide high-density information and minimize unnecessary fluff, catering to power users.

## Visual Identity
- **Modern & Minimal:** The interface should feature clean lines and ample whitespace, focusing on content and functionality over excessive decoration.
- **Functional & Dense:** Aim for high information density with dashboard-style layouts and data-rich visualizations, ensuring all critical agent data is visible.
- **Tactile & Premium:** Use multi-layered drop shadows and subtle noise textures to create a sense of depth and a high-end, "IDE-like" feel.
- **Dynamic & Harmonious:** Implement Material 3 principles with dynamic color schemes to ensure a cohesive and visually pleasing experience.

## Interaction Design
- **Keyboard Centric:** Design for efficiency by incorporating robust keyboard shortcuts and an intuitive command palette.
- **Visual Feedback:** Provide clear, immediate visual indicators for all system states, especially for live agent activities like terminal output and task progress.
- **Predictable Navigation:** Ensure consistent and intuitive routing that mirrors familiar developer structures like file systems or project hierarchies.
